I'm restarting my blog. It's been a while since I've published anything here, and it's time to change that. I've moved from Ghost to GitHub Pages, and I'm excited about what's ahead.

## Why the move

Ghost is a solid platform with a clean editor. I was using it as a SaaS, so there was really nothing to maintain. But it's been years since my last post, and the way I write has changed. I spend most of my day in VS Code, I draft everything in Markdown, and I lean on Copilot constantly.

Getting back into blogging needed a push, and working with Copilot and AI-driven development gave me one. There's plenty to write about, and the tooling makes the process enjoyable again. I just needed my blog to fit the workflow I already have, not the other way around.

I plan to write about the topics I work with day to day: Azure, AI, Kubernetes, cloud-native architecture, and developer tooling.
